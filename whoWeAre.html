Leigh Treat | President

I have worked for the Hays County Sheriff’s Office since 2011 and in 2021, it proved to be one of the most difficult for the Sheriff’s Office family. During the year, we lost 3 others to illnesses, before we lost Greg. Greg was aware of this and after he became sick, his wish was to come up with a way to help others during these extreme unforeseen instances. There were multiple fundraisers for each person at different times, while they were dealing with their medical issues. The Hays County Sheriff’s Office family has always been willing to help each other but sometimes we found it difficult to coordinate everyone. We are now able to pool our resources together, in one location, to help anyone at the Hays County Sheriff’s Office, with the Mitchum Assistance Foundation.
